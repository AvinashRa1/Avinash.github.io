Introduction to our Shop

The products from our store are produced with handpicked fresh green tea leaves off the tea plantations of the misty hill country of Sri Lanka. We source our products from a Single-origin as unblended teas, so that we can consistently guarantee the quality to our tea tasters. We only sell unflavored teas that are properly graded to industry standards in strict hygienic conditions. And our products are made of fresh teas from recent flushes, not teas that have been on shelves or warehouses for months. Our orders are packed only once the order has been confirmed and dispatched directly from Sri Lanka.

CEYLON: The first tea bushes were planted in Ceylon (now Sri Lanka) in 1867. “Ceylon” tea refers to the black tea produced in Sri Lanka. Sri Lanka’s tea gardens are spread across different climates and elevations – from cool and mountainous to humid and tropical. Ceylon teas can vary depending on where they grow, but they are generally known to be strong and brisk with a hint of spice. Sri Lanka produces a large portion of higher quality black teas exported into the world market.
