Support a Student, Strengthen a Community by Tea Leaf Trust

Summary
Education is essential in empowering young people from tea estate communities in Sri Lanka to expand their career prospects, realise their potential and help lift their families out of extreme poverty. This is the purpose of our Tea Leaf Centres - professional development and vocational training centres that provide young people between the ages of 18 and 26 years old with free education. With the current situation in Sri Lanka, this work has never been more important.
Long-Term Impact
When a student graduates from Tea Leaf they will be able to gain meaningful, full-time employment, double their household income and help lift their family out of financial hardship and become leaders in their community to drive positive change. By supporting a student to achieve their ambitions, you're also supporting at least 15 community members to have better lives, and in turn, strengthening a community!
